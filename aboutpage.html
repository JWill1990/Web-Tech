About uPd8

uPd8 was formed as a collaborative effort between 2 University of Bristol Computer Science students.

The motive behind the development of uPd8 was centered upon our belief that web users should be able to easily keep updated on the progress of projects they're interested in, find the latest issues of their favourite comic and book series, or whatever else they're interested in. Gone are the days of constantly having to search through your bookmarks on the off-chance of finding the latest issue!

We hope you find this a useful application for your hobbyist needs!
